ABOUT

Dragon Ball: Beyond Legacy

A 2D tile-based Dragon Ball inspired game built with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.

Features:

  • Choose from 3 unique classes
  • Explore a tile-based world
  • Battle enemies and power up
  • Collect all 7 Dragon Balls
  • Full mobile and desktop support

Created as a tribute to Dragon Ball and classic tile-based RPGs.
