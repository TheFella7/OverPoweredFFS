GAMEPLAY

  • Explore the open world to find enemies, items, and quests
  • Defeat enemies to gain experience and increase your power level
  • Charge your Ki (Q key) before using powerful attacks
  • Each race has unique abilities and transformations
  • Watch your health and Ki bars during combat
  • Collect Dragon Balls to summon Shenron and make a wish
  • Complete quests for special rewards

RACE ABILITIES

Saiyan: Zenkai Boost (heal after near-death), Super Saiyan transformations
Human: Fast learning, Spirit Bomb, high technique mastery
Namekian: Regeneration, stretch limbs, create objects
Android: Infinite energy, absorb attacks, no Ki drain
Frieza Race: Natural power, transformations, survive in space
